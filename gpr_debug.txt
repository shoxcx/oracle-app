What are Global Power Rankings?
GPR is a ranking of team strength in Tier 1 play. It is calculated using a mix of the following scores:
Team Performance
Context of Play
Ex: Worlds Playoffs > Regular Season
Recent Performance
Teams' latest matches matter more
In-Game Execution
Kills, Gold, & Game Dominance
Strength of Opponent
Difference in Power Score
Regional Strength Score
