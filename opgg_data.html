AI tips summary
Beta
Ahri is a utility champion, not a pure damage dealer. Focus on utilizing her ultimate's mobility for positioning and Charm for playmaking and CC support for your team. Rather than aggressive trades in lane, quickly clear waves with Q and impact other lanes or the jungle through roaming to unlock the game. Ahri's damage against tanks is low; prioritize utility and ability haste items like Malignance to provide consistent CC support.
